Pancakes

These pancakes are fluffy and easy to make. Perfect for breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  4. Serve warm with syrup or fruit.
