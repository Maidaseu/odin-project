Lasagna

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, cheese, and rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, cheese, and meat in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  5. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
